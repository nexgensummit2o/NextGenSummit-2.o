About The Event

We are back with three power-packed days of innovation, learning, and creativity! NextGen Summit 2.0, in collaboration with Microcare Academy and ITLU MEE, is designed to empower students with skills and opportunities. This event features a special talk by our proud alumnus, a Google-led workshop, a national-level hackathon, and a chance to get mentored for the Smart India Hackathon (SIH).

Why Participate?

Gain Skills

Master AI tools in a workshop led by Google. Learn to brainstorm, prototype, and present innovative solutions.

Network with Experts

Connect with industry leaders from Oracle, Accenture, and Microcare Solutions. Get valuable feedback and insights.

Mentorship for SIH

The best teams from our hackathon will be directly recommended and mentored for the National Level Smart India Hackathon (SIH).
